About Us

Mansons Guitar Shop

Mansons Guitar Shop can proudly boast one of the most experienced independent guitar stores in the world!

Ordering from Mansons online, by telephone or by visiting our vast custom designed guitar store in the heart of Exeter you can be assured of a guitar experience second to none. Unlike some competitors you will find our prices identical whether you visit or order via mail order.

We have operated since 1992 as a retail outlet and have a sales team happy to assist you on any purchase. Our team is headed by guitar luthier Hugh Manson and bass expert Adrian Ashton. We employ a further 12 staff including three world-tour experienced guitar technicians who can repair, modify or service your guitar for the ultimate in tone and feel. Service and warranty work is carried out in house or by a swift workshop team.

Every employee is a player and lover of guitars first and foremost.

Custom orders and Works guitars featuring our signature finishes such as aluminium swirls, mirror tops and sparkle finishes have also formed part of the core product range. Expansion of the official signature guitar models for Matt Bellamy of Muse to include our MB and DL variations also joined the existing models. Our user list continued to expand with players from every musical style such as Dave Grohl, Nick Beggs (Steve Hackett), Baz Warne (The Stranglers), Noisemaker (Japan), Seasick Steve, James Leach (SikTh) and Simon Neil (Biffy Clyro) are just some of the artists enjoying Manson instruments.

We are an official Gibson service centre and a Silver Authorised Taylor Repair Centre. You'll also find a client list that reflects the quality, artistic vision and professionalism of Manson's here at our Artist section.

Some of our clients include - Muse, Led Zeppelin, Them Crooked Vultures, Yes, Oasis, St.

Customers can come to the premises here by appointment and we can sit down with them and talk about design details, woods used, pickups and electronics.
