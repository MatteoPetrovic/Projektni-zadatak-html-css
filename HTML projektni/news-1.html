Manson MA 2020 Red Santa Limited Edition

Manson Guitar Works are proud to release the MA Red Santa Winter Limited Edition.

Following the hugely successful Bluebell Limited Run of 2019, the MA EVO Red Santa 2020 celebrates one of our most iconic guitar finishes with a Metallic Holosparkle Red gloss paint finish as first seen on Matthew Bellamy’s custom Manson instruments.

Those personal instruments of Bellamy carried several fan nicknames such the Glitterati, and, more notably, the Manson Santa which is the title introduced for this limited run.

The award winning MA model features are as before with custom Manson PF pickups made at our Ashburton workshops, solid shaft CTS Push Pull potentiometers, Manson custom hardware and established Made in the UK status.

Every edition comes with the coil tap option as standard and MIDI versions have a new, high-polish aluminium surround.


Prices Start at £1679 inc VAT. Available from Manson Guitar Works and our International Dealer Network.
